# BSA Translator Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** $(Get-Date -Format "yyyy-MM-dd")

## 1. Introduction

BSA Translator ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our translation services.

By using BSA Translator, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Data
- **Voice Audio**: Temporary voice recordings for speech recognition and translation
- **Text Input**: Text content you enter for translation purposes
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and language settings

### 2.2 Usage Data
- App functionality usage patterns
- Feature preferences and settings
- Crash reports and performance metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:
- **Translation Services**: Providing accurate speech-to-text and text translation
- **Service Improvement**: Enhancing app performance and user experience
- **Technical Support**: Troubleshooting issues and providing customer assistance
- **App Maintenance**: Ensuring app stability and security

## 4. Data Processing and Storage

### 4.1 Voice Data Processing
- Voice recordings are processed in real-time on your device
- Audio data is temporarily cached during processing and automatically deleted
- No permanent storage of voice recordings on our servers

### 4.2 Text Data
- Translation inputs are processed through translation APIs
- Text data may be cached locally for performance optimization
- Users can clear translation history from app settings

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Voice data: Deleted immediately after processing
- Translation cache: Stored locally, user-controllable
- Analytics data: Anonymous data retained for 24 months

## 5. Third-Party Services

BSA Translator integrates with the following services:

- **OpenAI Whisper**: For speech recognition functionality
- **Meta NLLB**: For translation services
- **Google Text-to-Speech**: Optional TTS engine (if selected by user)

These services may process data according to their respective privacy policies.

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data, including:
- Local processing of sensitive data where possible
- Secure transmission protocols for API communications
- Regular security assessments and updates

## 7. User Rights and Controls

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of stored data
- Opt-out of non-essential data collection
- Control app permissions through device settings
- Clear app cache and translation history

## 8. International Data Transfers

As a translation app, data may be processed in various countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

## 9. Children's Privacy

BSA Translator is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

- Requiring acceptance of updated policy for continued use
